Welcome to PictureWaves!
Make sure to have your sound on.
Start drawing! Choose from a selection of gradients or your own custom color.
Adjust the brush width and the tone with the slider!
Each stroke will produce a sound depending on where it is and how long the stroke is.
Click the play button to hear your masterpiece! It will play the notes in the order you drew it.
